Year

2017

Client

Internal

Mission

Driving participation in eLearning through an immersive multi format game experience.

Notes

I was tasked with creating a method of driving participation in an eLearning platform. I opted to create a gamification experience, drawing in email comms, prebaked quiz functionality and my own in-browser game.

Users were led into the game with an email, which led to a chatbot. The bot conversation was designed to elicit responses which could be used to deepen immersion later in the game.

Another email was sent after the chat to draw users into a hotspot game, where users had to click an image and enter the name of the client.
